About Us

FOSSMEC (Free and Open Source Software Cell at Model Engineering College, Kochi) is a vibrant community of open-source enthusiasts committed to fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation. Established to integrate the college's technical endeavors, FOSSMEC provides a structured environment for students to engage with and contribute to the global open-source ecosystem.

Our mission is to promote the use and development of free and open-source software (FOSS) among students and the broader community. Through regular workshops, training programs, and events like DebUtsav and Code-A-Pookalam, we aim to educate and inspire individuals to explore the benefits of FOSS and actively participate in its growth.

As a non-profit organization, FOSSMEC is dedicated to providing resources and opportunities for students to enhance their skills, collaborate on projects, and contribute to meaningful technological advancements. By nurturing a collaborative culture, we strive to empower the next generation of innovators and leaders in the open-source community.
